Enchiladas

Life is like an Enchilada, its amzing!!

This delicious recipe will fill your day with joy and excite the taste buds in your mouth. Enchiladas are great for any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Cheese
  • Chicken or Beef
  • Onions
  • Enchilada Sauce

Directions

  1. warm up tortillas
  2. Warm up chicken of beef to you liking
  3. Prepare red or green enchilada sauce
  4. Saute onions
  5. Stuff tortillas with meat and onions
  6. place all rolled enchiladas in a casserole dish
  7. Top the dish with plenty of sauce and cheese
  8. Cook in oven at 350 degrees fahrenheit 30 min
