French Toast

Compliment Your day with some delicious French Toast

This delicious plate is a nice treat for the family. The tasty and aromatic combination that French toast delivers will surely brings smiles to the table.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Brioche Bread
  • Cinnamon
  • Powder Sugar
  • Strawberries and Bananas
  • Maple Syrup

Directions

  1. Crack five to six eggs and mix
  2. warm skillet and apply butter to prevent Toast from sticking
  3. Soak bread in egg
  4. place on skillet and let cook until golden brown appearance
  5. chop Strawberries and Bananas
  6. Top French Toast with fruit, powder sugar and cinnamon
  7. Apply Maple Syrup to your liking
